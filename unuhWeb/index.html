ELIZABETH TERRY
FOUNDER

Against a professional background of training, HRD, ICT4D, and project management, Elizabeth pursued training and certification in the field of Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP), to help deepen her own transformation. This led her to the work in which she is currently passionately engaged – NLP training and coaching for individuals, youth, professionals, and leaders.

GLEN HENRY
DESIGNER & DEVELOPER

An experienced app developer, and creative professional, with a strong background in management. He is seeking to expand the way Jamaicans explore and interact with media. He founded Spritewrench Studios, a Kingston based game development business focused on developing narrative games in 2019 and has been a member of the Jamaica Game Developer Society since 2013.

MICHELE GEISTER
CONTENT DIRECTOR

A proven, creative force across multi-media platforms. Her extensive career has included the roles of creator/producer/director and feature writer of iconic content for television, radio and print, on national networks, both in Canada & Jamaica. She is the creator and original director of Jamaica's long-running morning show, "Smile Jamaica". Michele is considered a pioneer in Canada for her contributions towards the development of the Canadian Hip Hop music industry through her internationally syndicated series: Soul in the City and RapCity.

JASON SCOTT
PROJECT MANAGER

A digital transformation specialist with strong commercialization, marketing and project management experience for digital solutions. He is also Deputy President for the Jamaica Computer Society. Jason provides overall technical and marketing advice as well as technical project coordination to the team.

RYAN LINCOLN
UX DESIGN RESEARCHER

A UX Designer with over 13 years’ experience in the software development industry. With a background in software engineering, worked as both developer and designer on mobile and web projects for clients throughout the US, Canada, and the Caribbean. A strong proponent for human-centered design (HCD), bringing a strong understanding of consumer psychology, business processes, marketing, and design principles to a holistic development approach.
Very passionate about products designed and the people that use them - The goal is to ensure that every product or application strikes that perfect balance between the business and the users’ needs. Always looking for ways to grow, learn and experiment with new ideas.
