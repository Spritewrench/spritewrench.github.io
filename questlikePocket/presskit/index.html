Description

Questlike: Pocket sees players take on the role of a hunter, tracking beasts hiding in real-world locations.
Battle, Craft powerful gear and Strive to become the world top ranked hunter!

Game Play Features
  • Attack and Defend with a Timed Combat System
  • Hunt monsters in different biomes based on real-world locations
  • Craft unique weapons and potions for Strategic Battles
