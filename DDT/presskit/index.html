Description

Help Tashia unravel the case of a heinous murder and find her cell phone in this point & click, mystery adventure based on Caribbean folklore!

Current Features:
  • Explore a Hidden World! - Navigate a beautiful world that blends the magical with the mundane. Explore the various vending stalls as you conduct your investigation. Visit each location at different times throughout the day as the suspects move about.
  • Investigate the Halfway Market! - A magical place populated by Duppy, mischievous spirits who trick and teach in equal measure. Collect clues, uncover mysterious motives and confirm which suspect has the means to conduct this heinous caper!
  • Solve the Mystery! - Interrogate 7 suspects based on creatures of Myth & Legend. Cross reference statements based on the facts you've gathered and deduce the culprit responsible!
  • Compounding Curse System - As you gather cursed treasure, your crew grows increasingly afflicted. Deploying afflicted crew members raises your curse meter, and if full by the end of a round, you spin the ROULETTE OF DOOM!.
