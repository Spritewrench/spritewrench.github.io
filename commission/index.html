2021 Commission Pricing

These prices are for illustrations of one full-bodied/bust character with a simple background (no complex scenes).
All payments are to be made via paypal.
Payment plan structures are outlined below.

Info

Each commission will include a contract outlining fair use, contractor obligations & expectations. This contract is to be signed by both parties before commencement of work.

Process

The work process begins with a (digital) rough black+white sketch based on the instruction/guidance communicated. Once the sketch has been confirmed I will continue with the final piece.
Feedback will be gathered during the checkin periods outlined.
If you are not entirely comfortable with the sketch, you are free to request changes according to the limits outlined for each commission type.

Delivery

Each commission will be delivered via email and will include a zip file containing the final PNG and PSD files.
