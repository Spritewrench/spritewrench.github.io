What is the Game?

Duppies and Gunmen is a tabletop role-playing game in an alternate present-day setting where myth is a reality. You and your squad work together to overcome challenges, mundane and supernatural.

Designed for extended campaigns or bite sized one-shots, our brand new game system creates a space where Caribbean people can tell stories their way, full of vibes and flavour!
