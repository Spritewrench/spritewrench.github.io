About Us

Table Top Jamaica is a trio of board game and table top enthusiasts formed in 2023.
Andre Smith (Creator) , Alistair Douglas and Zoe Gordon (Senior Game Developers).
We are proud to be Jamaican and are excited for the future of table-top roleplaying games in Jamaica, the Caribbean and Worldwide.

Duppies and Gunmen

Jamaica’s first roleplaying game of its kind!
Duppies and Gunmen is a tabletop role-playing game in an alternate present-day setting where myth is a reality. You and your squad work together to overcome challenges, mundane and supernatural.
